# veu-auth: 24-Word Recovery (BIP-39)

Veu is a **Zero-Account** protocol. There are no usernames or passwords stored on a server. Your entire digital existence—your Circles, your Keys, and your Ghost Identity—is derived from a single **24-word seed phrase**.

## 🔐 Cryptographic Foundation
Veu follows the **BIP-39** standard for mnemonic seed generation and **BIP-32/44** for Hierarchical Deterministic (HD) key derivation.

### 1. Entropy & Generation
- **Source:** 256 bits of high-quality entropy generated by the iPhone's **Secure Enclave** (Apple's `SecRandomCopyBytes`).
- **Checksum:** An 8-bit checksum is appended to the entropy.
- **Mnemonic:** The 264 bits are mapped to 24 words from the standard BIP-39 English wordlist.

### 2. The Master Seed
- The mnemonic is salted with a user-defined (optional) **Passphrase** to produce a 512-bit Master Seed using PBKDF2 (HMAC-SHA512, 2048 iterations).

## 🌳 Derivation Paths (The Identity Tree)
Veu uses specific paths to separate different 'concerns' of your identity, ensuring that leaking one key does not compromise others.

| Component | Path | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Identity Master** | `m/44'/1111'/0'/0` | The root key for your Public Callsign. |
| **Identity Epochs** | `m/44'/1111'/0'/n` | Used for the **Identity Burn** mechanism (where 'n' is the epoch). |
| **Circle Handshakes** | `m/44'/1111'/1'/0` | Used to generate ephemeral keys for Emerald Handshakes. |
| **Backup Encryption** | `m/44'/1111'/2'/0` | Used to encrypt the local **Circle Ledger** for off-device backups. |

## 🛡️ Secure Enclave Integration
- **Key Generation:** Private keys are derived *inside* the Secure Enclave and marked as `non-exportable`.
- **Seed Input:** During recovery, the 24 words are entered into a dedicated, 'Secure Input' view that bypasses standard keyboard logging/autocorrect.
- **Biometric Guard:** Accessing the seed phrase or performing a sensitive derivation requires **FaceID/TouchID** authentication.

## 🚫 The 'Golden Rule' of Veu
**If you lose your 24 words, you lose your identity.**
Because Veu does not store your keys, there is no 'Forgot Password' flow. You are the sole custodian of your Double Mirror.
